SWRL rules

  • Rule1
  • Rule2
  • Rule3

Rule1back to ToC

Rule to make a Pilot Captain only when has more than 500 hours

Pilot c(?pilot) , hasFlightHours dp(?pilot,?hours) , greaterThan(?) -> hasPilotRange dp(?pilot,"Captain")

Rule2back to ToC

Rule to mark a Review as Verified only when the passenger who has written the review also has bought a ticket associated with a trip

Passenger c(?passenger) , hasBoughtA op(?passenger,?ticket) , hasWritten op(?passenger,?review) -> isVerified dp(?review,"true"^^boolean)

Rule3back to ToC

When a business passenger take a trip, the trip is mark as a Business Trip

Passenger c(?passenger) , hasBoughtA op(?passenger,?ticket) , isFor op(?ticket,?trip) , isTypeOfTraveller dp(?passenger,"Business") -> tripPurpose dp(?trip,"Business Trip")
